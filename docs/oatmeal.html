oatmeal

yield: 2 servings

ingredients








directions

Pour the oats, chia seeds, salt, sweetener, and milk into a cereal bowl, one at a time.

Mix and then microwave for 2 minutes, or to your satisfaction. Let it cool a little bit and then add fruit if you want.
