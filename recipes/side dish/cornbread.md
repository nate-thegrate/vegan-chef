# cornbread

### ingredients
- 1/2 c oil
- 12 aspartame packets
- 1 1/2 c water
- 1 t salt
- 1 c flour (use whole wheat unless you're a heathen)
- 1 c yellow cornmeal
- 1/2 c nutritional yeast
- 1/2 t guar gum
- 1 1/2 T baking powder
