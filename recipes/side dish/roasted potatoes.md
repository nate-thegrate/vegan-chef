# roasted potatoes

### ingredients
- 8 potatoes
- 1/4 cup oil
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon garlic
- 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
- 1 teaspoon rosemary

<br>

### directions:

Wash & cube the potatoes.

Mix oil, salt, and spices in a bowl, then pour mixture onto potatoes.

Bake at 425Â°F for 20 min or until potatoes reach optimal crispiness.
