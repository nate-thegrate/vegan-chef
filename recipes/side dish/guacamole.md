# guacamole

### ingredients
- 3 avocados
- 1/8 cup lime juice
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 red onion
- 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon cumin
- 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

<br>

### directions:

Remove the avocado peels & pits, then mash them and mix in the lime juice and salt.

Add everything else and mix.

Refrigerate for about an hour and serve.
