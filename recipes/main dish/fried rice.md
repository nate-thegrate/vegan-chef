# fried rice

### ingredients
- 1 onion
- 14 oz tofu
- 3 T oil
- 1 t sesame oil
- 1/2 t garlic powder
- salt & black pepper to taste
- 4 c cooked rice
- 1 c matchstick carrots
- 1 chopped bell pepper
- 1/2 c peas
- 3 T soy sauce
- 1 T oyster sauce
- 1 green onion

<br>

### directions:

Dice the onion and slice the tofu into cubes. Add both to a skillet over medium-high heat, along with oil, sesame oil, garlic, salt, and pepper, and cook for about 4 minutes.

Add in rice, carrots, pepper, peas, soy sauce, and oyster sauce; saut√© for 3-4 minutes.

Top with sliced green onion and serve.
