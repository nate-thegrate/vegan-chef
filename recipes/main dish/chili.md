# chili

### ingredients
- 4 c dry beans
- 4 cans diced tomatoes
- 4 cans tomato sauce
- 1 1/2 lb plant-based ground beef
- 1 green pepper
- 1 onion
- 2 tsp minced garlic
- 2 tbsp chili powder
- 1 tsp pepper
- 2 tsp cumin
- 2 tsp basil
- 8 tsp beef base

<br>

### directions:

Combine everything in a Crock Pot or insta-pot. Cook for who knows how long.
