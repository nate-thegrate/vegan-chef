# rice pudding

### ingredients
- 3/4 c rice
- 1 1/2 c soy milk
- 1/4 t salt
- 16 Splenda packets
- 1/4 c oat fiber
- 1/4 t molasses
- 1/2 T vanilla
- 1/8 t cinnamon

<br>

### directions:

Combine all ingredients and cook them however you normally like to cook rice. (A rice maker or insta-pot works well for this.)
