# rice pudding

### ingredients
- 1 c soy milk
- 1/4 c oat fiber
- 2 c cooked rice
- 1/4 t salt
- 16 Splenda packets
- 1/4 t molasses
- 1/2 T vanilla
- 1/8 t cinnamon

<br>

### directions:

Mix oat fiber & soy milk together with a whisk. Then add everything to a pot and simmer for 10 min (or until desired consistency is reached).
