# french toast

### ingredients
- 1/2 c soy milk
- 1/2 T vanilla
- 1/4 t salt
- 1/8 t guar gum
- sliced bread

<br>

### directions:

Mix ingredients together.

Dunk bread in mixture and then cook it like a pancake.

Sprinkle cinnamon on the french toast right as it starts cooking for a nice boost to flavor & aesthetic.
