# pancakes

### Ingredients
- 1 c water
- 1/2 t salt
- 3 aspartame packets
- 2 T oil
- 1 cup whole wheat flour
- 2 t baking powder
- 1/8 t guar gum
- sourdough starter (optional)

<br>

### Directions:

Add the salt & aspartame to the water, then add everything else and mix.
