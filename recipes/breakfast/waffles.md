# waffles
*yield: 3 servings*

### ingredients
- 1 cup water
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 cup oil
- 2/3 cup sourdough starter
- 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
- 1/4 cup lupin flour
- 1/2 tablespoon baking powder
- 1/8 teaspoon guar gum

<br>

### directions:

Mix everything in a bowl and cook as per your waffle maker's instructions.
