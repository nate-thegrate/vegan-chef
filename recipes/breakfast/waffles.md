# waffles

*yield: 2 servings*

### ingredients
- 1 c water
- 1/2 t salt
- 1/3 c oil
- 1/4 c sourdough starter
- 3/4 c whole wheat flour
- 1/2 T baking powder
- 1/8 t guar gum

<br>

### directions:

Mix everything in a bowl and cook as per your waffle maker's instructions.
