# waffles

### ingredients
- 1 c water
- 1/2 t salt
- 1 T erythritol
- 1/3 c sourdough starter
- 1/4 c oil
- 3/4 cup whole wheat flour
- 1/2 T baking powder
- 1/8 t guar gum

<br>

### directions:

Add the salt, aspartame, and sourdough to the water, then add everything else and mix.

Cook the waffle batter according to your waffle maker's instructions.
