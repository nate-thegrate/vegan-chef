# waffles

### Ingredients
- 1 c water
- 1/2 t salt
- 1 aspartame packet
- 1/4 c oil
- 1 cup whole wheat flour
- 2 t baking powder
- 1/8 t guar gum
- sourdough starter (optional)

<br>

### Directions:

Add the salt & aspartame to the water, then add everything else and mix.

Cook the waffle batter according to your waffle maker's instructions.
