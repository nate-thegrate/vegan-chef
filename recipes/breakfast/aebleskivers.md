# aebleskivers
*yield: 3 servings*

### ingredients
- 1 1/2 c water
- 1/2 t salt
- 1 t erythritol
- 1/4 c sourdough starter
- 1 c whole wheat flour
- 1 t baking powder
- 1/8 t guar gum

<br>

### directions:

Combine all ingredients, then cook in an aebleskiver pan.
