# aebleskivers
*yield: 3 servings*

### ingredients
- 1 1/2 cup water
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon erythritol
- 1/3 cup sourdough starter
- 1 cup whole wheat flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/8 teaspoon guar gum
- 1/3 cup oil

<br>

### directions:

Combine all ingredients, **except for the oil.**

Cook in an aebleskiver pan (add ~Â¼ teaspoon of oil to each divet before pouring the batter).
