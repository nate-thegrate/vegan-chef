# german pancakes
*yield: 2 servings*

### ingredients
- 1 cup soy milk
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon oil
- 1/3 cup sourdough starter
- 3/4 cup white flour
- 1/3 cup lupin flour
- 2 tablespoons wheat gluten
- 1/4 teaspoon guar gum
