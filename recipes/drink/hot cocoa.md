# hot cocoa
*yield: 2 servings*

### ingredients
- 1/4 cup cocoa powder
- 1/4 teaspoon sunflower lecithin
- 1/8 teaspoon salt
- 2 cups soy milk
- 1/4 teaspoon sucralose
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1/8 cup oil

<br>

### directions:

Mix dry ingredients (cocoa, lecithin, and salt) together, then add everything else.

Heat in the microwave or on the stove.
